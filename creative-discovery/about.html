The host

Joachim Bekkevold

Host of Creative Discovery — candid conversations about business, investing, and life in Cambodia.

I didn't plan to end up in Cambodia. I came for a few weeks and stayed for years — and in that time I met the most interesting people I've ever come across: founders building from nothing, investors spotting things the rest of the world hadn't, expats who'd quietly figured out a better life.

Creative Discovery started because those conversations were too good to keep to myself. Every week I sit down with someone who's actually doing something here — building a company, making an investment, navigating a life in a country most people only know from temples and tuk-tuks — and I ask them to share what they've learned. The real version, not the polished one.

This isn't a show about Cambodia as a tourist destination. It's about Cambodia as a place where smart, ambitious people are building real things. And if you're thinking about doing the same — or you're just curious about what that looks like — this is the show for you.

I believe the best conversations happen when people are generous with what they know. That's what I ask of every guest, and it's what I try to give back through this show.

"The best conversations happen when people are generous with what they know."

What the show stands for

Real over polished

We'd rather hear about the deal that almost fell apart than the highlight reel. Honesty makes better content and better decisions.

On-the-ground over theoretical

Every guest on this show is actually here, doing the thing. No armchair experts, no secondhand advice.

Generous over gatekept

The information that helps people build, invest, and live well shouldn't be locked behind closed doors. We share openly.
